How To Play

Welcome to Avenger's Snap!
The memory game based on your favourite Marvel Avengers characters!

The aim of the game is simple, match two cards via flipping them on the game board.
Once all cards are flipped - the game is complete!
Aim to complete the game in as few moves and as short a time as possible!
